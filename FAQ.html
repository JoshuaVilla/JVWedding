FAQs

Is the wedding indoors or outdoors?

Our beautiful wedding is outdoors. Please plan accordingly to the weather, because it is February and in the winter time.


What is the dress code?

Wear something that is cocktail to semi-formal/dressy casual attire.


Will there be alcohol?

Yes so please party and drink responsibly.


Can I use my phone during the wedding?

There will be a "No Phone" policy at the ceremony as we will have our lovely photographer and videographer to capture the moment. However, at the reception, you are allowed to take any photos and videos your heart desires! Be sure to use the hashtag "#ANguyen4Villanuevas" on any social media posts.


When is the RSVP deadline?

January 8th, 2024


Can I bring a date/plus one?

Due to limited capacity, we can't accommodate additional guests and can only allow the people specifically named on the invitation.


Can I bring my kid(s)?

Our wedding will be adult-only, besides our Olivia Joy and family members, but if you have any concerns, please don't hesitate to reach out. We will work it out together.
